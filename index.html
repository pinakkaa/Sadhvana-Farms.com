PROJECT USP'S

Generous plot sizes

Starting from 6,000 sq. ft., each farmland plot offers ample space to create your own private retreat amidst nature. Whether you envision fruit plantations, organic farming, weekend getaways, or a custom farmhouse, the generous plot dimensions provide flexibility, privacy, and long-term value appreciation.

Hassle free managment

Owning farmland has never been easier. Sadhvana Farms Estates takes care of complete farm maintenance, including plantation management, irrigation systems, security, landscaping, and routine upkeep. This allows investors and landowners to enjoy the benefits of farmland ownership without the day-to-day operational responsibilities.

Quick returns

The project is designed to generate sustainable agricultural income through carefully planned plantations and crop cultivation. With diversified farming practices and professional management, investors can expect periodic returns from harvest cycles while simultaneously benefiting from the long-term appreciation of their land asset.

Comprehensive ammenities

Every plot comes with thoughtfully planned infrastructure and lifestyle amenities, including secure fencing, internal roads, electricity, drip irrigation, water management systems, clubhouse access, parking facilities, landscaped green spaces, and a variety of fruit-bearing, flowering, and forest trees for a complete farm-living experience.
